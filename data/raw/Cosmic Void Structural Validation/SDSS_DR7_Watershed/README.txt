Recommended use
---------------
Alternative non-spherical public void catalog for robustness checks on void-definition dependence

Main files or products
----------------------
Catalog products described in Sutter et al. 2012 and later public watershed-based SDSS DR7 releases

Key columns or quantities
-------------------------
Effective radius; redshift; member zones/cells; radial profile products (catalog-dependent)
