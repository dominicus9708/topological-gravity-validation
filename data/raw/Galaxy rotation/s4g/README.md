# S4G data

## Description
S4G (Spitzer Survey of Stellar Structure in Galaxies) is a deep 3.6 and 4.5 μm imaging survey of nearby galaxies, widely used for structural parameters and infrared morphology.

## Core references
Sheth, K., Regan, M., Hinz, J. L., et al. (2010),
"The Spitzer Survey of Stellar Structure in Galaxies (S4G)",
Publications of the Astronomical Society of the Pacific, 122, 1397.

Salo, H., Laurikainen, E., Laine, J., et al. (2015),
"Spitzer Survey of Stellar Structure in Galaxies. Survey overview and structural decompositions",
The Astrophysical Journal Supplement Series, 219, 4.

## Use in this project
Used mainly as a structural/photometric support source and as a naming or crossmatch support layer.
