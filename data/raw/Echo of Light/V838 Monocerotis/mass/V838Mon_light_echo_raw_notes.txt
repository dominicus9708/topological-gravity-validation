V838Mon_light_echo_raw_notes.txt
================================

Direct light-echo baseline source
---------------------------------
[LE-01] V838 Mon: light echo evolution and distance estimate
- Most directly useful paper for 2002-2004 light echo geometry and time evolution
- Covers images from 2002 May to 2004 December
- Measures radius evolution and echo-centre shift
- Compares thin-sheet and thin-shell dust models
- Favors a thin interstellar sheet interpretation over a circumstellar shell
- Useful as the main standard-reference paper for radius and centre/offset comparisons

Public explanatory source
-------------------------
[LE-02] NASA Light Echoes from V838 Mon
- Useful for non-technical framing
- States clearly that what looks like outwardly moving structure is a light echo in ambient dust
- Good source for explaining why apparent expansion is not literal matter ejection at that apparent speed

Epoch image support
-------------------
[LE-03] NASA/Hubble epoch image references
- Useful for keeping a visual time-series reference for 2002-2004
- Good companion material for raw folders when comparing morphology by epoch

How to use in the project
-------------------------
1. LE-01 = baseline science reference
2. LE-02 = explanatory framing reference
3. LE-03 = image-series support reference
